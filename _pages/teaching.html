Class
  1. 2022~2024, CSE 40403 Geotechnical design, for 150~200 Year 2 or 3 Undergraduate students

    2. Topic 1. Site investigation

    Introduction and Geotechnical Properties of Soil (in lab), Natural Soil Deposits and Subsoil Exploration (in field)

    Topic 2. Slope Stability

    Factor of safety; Translational sliding failure; LEM-based method of slices; Design of slope stability

    Topic 3. Shallow Foundations

    Key points of a foundation design; Ultimate Bearing Capacity (ULS); Settlement (SLS)

    Topic 4. Mat (Raft) Foundations

    From combined footings to Mat Foundation; Bearing capacity; Settlement; Compensated foundation

    Topic 5. Pile Foundations

    Single pile: Pile loading capacity, Point load capacity, Frictional resistance, Pile load test, Settlement;

    Pile group: Configuration & group efficiency; Ultimate capacity; Settlement

    Topic 6. Earth Retaining Structures

    Retaining Walls: Lateral earth pressure theories, Stability, Gravity and cantilever walls, Mechanically stabilized retaining walls;

    Sheet Pile Walls: Cantilever sheet pile walls, Anchored sheet-pile walls, Penetration;

    Braced Cuts: Pressure envelope, Design of components, Stability and deformation
